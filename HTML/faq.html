We all have questions, heres a few of the more common ones were asked!

What is the cost going the be?

Our most frequent question, and probably the most difficult to answer without seeing the project first hand. Typically we fall between $30-$50/sqft but this is all depending on the desired finish, size of the project, If a kitchen is included or if a walkout is needed. As you an see there are lots of factors. We would love to come give you a free quote and get you an accurate number for your project. reach out to us on our contact us page!

How soon can you get started?

This is a great question, and one asked very often! There are a couple factors at play with starting time, number one is the deposit from the customer. Once the deposit is recieved a spot in our schedule is solidified, at that point we are usually a few weeks to a few months booked out on our schedule. For more accurate times and information reach out to us from our contact us page!

How will the fluctuating market affect my project?

With the ongoing economic changes, knowing what the future holds can be daunting. But our normal projects are fixed price arrangments, meaning as long as you accept our quote in the given ammount of time, that is a fixed price. The only changes to that are determined by extras you as the owner would like to add or remove to the project!

Do you take credit cards?

Currently we do not accept credit card payments, but who knows what the future may hold!

Do you finance projects?

Sadly at this time we do not finance projects, but who knows what the future may hold!

Why T-squared Construction?

We like to say were the best around! We do our very best to listen to your wants/needs and help your project come to life, but don't take our word for it! check out our about us page to learn more about us and take a look at our reviews!
